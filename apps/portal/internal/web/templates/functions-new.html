New Function

The Lambda moment, on your laptop: pick a source, name it, and the platform builds your image in-cluster (Argo Workflows + BuildKit → your Zot registry) and deploys it as a scale-to-zero function (a Knative Service). Modules 06 and 07 each taught one half; this form does both from one POST — no CLI, no cloud.

{{/* The two-step it kicks off, spelled out — this is a teaching console, so the mechanism is never hidden. */}}
  1. Build — an Argo Workflow in namespace builds clones the source, runs BuildKit, and pushes fn-<name>:v1 to Zot. Track it on Builds.
  2. Deploy — a Knative Service fn-<name> in namespace demo points at that image. It waits in ImagePullBackOff until the build lands, then goes Ready on its own. The URL shows up on Services.
