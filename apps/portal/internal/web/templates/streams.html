JetStream streams on your NATS server — durable messaging you can inspect. The capstone's in-memory broker forgets everything on restart; a stream persists messages to disk, and each consumer tracks its own position through them. This is a read-only view straight off the NATS monitoring endpoint — one GET /jsz, no client library.
